About

Landing in Warsaw puts you at the center of all attractions in Poland due to its central location. Getting cheap car rentals in Warsaw provides a good base for taking day trips from the city at your convenience. Drive about 1h 25min outside Warsaw to explore the Mazovia region, around 55 miles away via the DK7 route. Travel to the tranquil Masurian Lake District is also more convenient with Warsaw car rentals. The district is roughly 207 miles, and the journey takes about 3h 20min via the S7. Take a day trip to Turon, a UNESCO World Heritage site, under 4h drive from Warsaw.
